Charitable Impact & Community Outreach

Dr. James believes in giving back to communities in need. Below is a showcase of medical supply contributions and charitable initiatives that have made a meaningful difference in healthcare accessibility for underserved populations.

Tupua Tamasese Meaole (TTM) Hospital Donation

July 16, 2025

Donated essential medical equipment including stethoscopes, blood pressure monitors, and diagnostic tools to Tupua Tamasese Meaole (TTM) Hospital, supporting their emergency department and general care facilities.

Impact: Enabled better patient diagnostics for a main hospital in Samoa, improving the quality of care in the emergency department of this hospital.

Hospital Militar Docente Dr. Ramón de Lara Donation

February 3, 2025

Organized and delivered basic medical supplies to Hospital Militar Docente Dr. Ramón de Lara in East Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic, including needles, gauzes, antibiotics, and other essential medical supplies.

Impact: Provided essential medical supplies supporting the hospital's daily operations and improving patient care capabilities.

Pediatric Care Initiative

October 22, 2025

Contributed specialized pediatric care supplies, including infant care kits, pediatric examination tools, and educational materials to children's hospital in Mexico City.

Impact: Enhanced pediatric care capabilities, benefiting over 300 children annually with improved diagnostic and treatment resources.

Mobile Health Clinic Support

August 10, 2025

Provided funding and medical supplies to support a mobile health clinic serving remote villages in Chiapas, enabling regular medical checkups and preventive care in areas with no permanent healthcare facilities.

Impact: Brought healthcare services to 12 remote villages, conducting health screenings for over 800 individuals who previously had no access to medical care.

Surgical Instruments Collection

June 5, 2025

Coordinated a collection of sterilized surgical instruments and supplies for Hospital Regional in Monterrey, supporting their surgical department with essential tools for various procedures.

Impact: Enabled the hospital to perform 50+ additional surgical procedures annually, reducing wait times for patients requiring urgent operations.

Medical Education Program

April 18, 2025

Donated medical textbooks, training manuals, and educational materials to a nursing school in Puebla, supporting the education of future healthcare professionals.

Impact: Provided learning resources for 150 nursing students, enhancing their education and preparing them for careers in healthcare.
